About Us

At Hermes Tutoring, we’re driven by the belief that students achieve their best when they’re taught with clarity, care, and strategy. Our mission is to transform challenges in math, science, and the humanities into opportunities for growth, empowering students to not just improve scores but to build the confidence and habits that carry into every aspect of life.

What makes us different is our people. Our tutors are top performers and students of top universities, with proven track records in standardized testing, advanced coursework, and college admissions guidance. They know firsthand the pressure students face and use that experience to create personalized, educational sessions. Whether it’s boosting an SAT/ACT score by hundreds of points, mastering AP-level material, or navigating the college application process, our team is committed to helping students reach, and even exceed, their goals.

We offer both one-on-one and group classes across a wide range of subjects: SAT/ACT prep, advanced mathematics, AP sciences, AP English, AP History & Humanities, and preparatory tutoring for younger learners. With flexible online scheduling and tailored teaching styles, we ensure every student receives the guidance they need to succeed. At Hermes Tutoring, success is more than a test score, it’s building the mindset and skill set for lifelong achievement.
