Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and use your information.

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

At Hermes Tutoring ("Company," "we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website www.hermestutoring.com.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services or website, we may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide through forms or sign-ups.
  • Payment Information: If you make a purchase, payment details are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store your complete credit/debit card details on our servers.
  • Automatically Collected Information: Browser type, device information, and website usage via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve tutoring services.
  • Communicate with you about your account, scheduling, or payments.
  • Process payments securely through Stripe.
  • Ensure website functionality and security.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All payments are processed using Stripe, a trusted, PCI-compliant payment processor.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your data only with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe) for transactions.
  • Service providers that help us operate the website (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

We do not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of communications at any time.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to improve website performance, user experience, and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Effective Date."

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
